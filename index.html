Acknowledgement of Country

We would like to acknowledge that we meet today on the land of the kaurna people, lands that we're stolen by the settlers and that where sovereignty was never seated, I ask that we pay respect to those past, present and unknown, that we acknowledge those whom have been mistreated, abused and/or killed for being a custodian of the land we meet on today and that we pause for a moment to ground ourselves so we can best take in this knowledge and have a better future for us all.
